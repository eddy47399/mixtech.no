What is OwnCloud?

OwnCloud is a file sharing server that puts the control and security of your own data back into your hands.
The most common compeditors is Dropbox or OneDrive that offer the same functions.

OwnCloud is available under an ‘open source license‘, which gives you the right to examine, share and modify it. ownCloud is developed by an international community of both paid and volunteer contributors
